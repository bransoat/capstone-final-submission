Frequently Asked Questions

What is Animetrics?

Animetrics is a company that uses animal boxes to record and share data.

What's the advantage to using an Animetrics box?

Animetrics boxes are excellent for recording data and minimizing time in the field.

How do the boxes work?

The boxes record the animals, take pictures of the animals, and record data about the animals.

Are Animetrics Boxes only for professional researchers?

No way! Institutions of all kinds are encouraged to use our product.
